📍 Rajasthan, India 📅 Feb–Mar 2023
Effect of pH on Growth and Chlorophyll Content of Chlorella

Investigated the effect of varying pH levels (5, 7, 9, 11) on the growth and chlorophyll content of Chlorella algae using Bold Basal Medium. Designed controlled experiments with duplicates, adjusting pH using 1N HCl and 1N KOH, and monitored natural variation over 18 days. Found that alkaline conditions (pH 9–11) significantly enhanced chlorophyll production, contributing to understanding of optimal algal growth conditions.
